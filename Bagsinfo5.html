Ngulsham

Like other bags in our collection, the Ngulsham attracts attention through its unique combination of colours, pattern and style. On a turquoise base shade of colour, beautiful traditional diamond-shaped pattern are woven, mainly in greens and reds. The outer material is mostly made of silk, which makes the bag very precious. With the overhead zipper, which goes practically over the entire width, the access to the undivided interior is very convenient.
This ladies’ bag is versatile due to its compactness and elegance. It offers enough space for necessary items that you wouldn’t like to miss.
