Wooden Bowls

Wooden Bowls Noble and chic! These artfully turned wooden bowls are part of the authentic Bhutan. Depending on their shape and size they are used in different ways. You can either serve Ara in them, the traditionally home-made hard drink, use them as soup cups or put them on the altar for offerings. We included them in our assortment because these wooden bowls are excellent incense stick holders. Filled with normal or coloured decorative sand, you can easily put in one or more incense sticks. While burning, the bowl catches the ashes.
