Bags




The mainly traditionally designed handbags and purses on our website are all handmade. The material of the outer shell is mostly high quality hand woven fabric. The beautiful and traditional patterns are often made from pure silk. The outer material of the more expensive bags is even made almost entirely of silk.

The woven fabrics that are used for these bags are produced in small-scale factories or they are made by freelance weavers. Tailors design different types of bag and purse and choose suitable materials. This individuality makes our bags special and unique.
